FAQs
What should the customer know about your pricing (e.g., discounts, fees)?



Our pricing will vary based on your needs, location and desired package.


What is your typical process for working with a new customer?


We want to get to know you. Whether it be a phone call or a cup of coffee, we want to know exactly what you envision for your event and how we can take care of all the entertainment so that you have one less thing to worry about. We will provide an event planning form for the client to complete and then will review it together. After that, we take care of the rest!


What types of customers have you worked with?


We regularly provide DJ services for schools, weddings, birthday parties, graduation parties, anniversary parties, corporate events and more.


Describe a recent project you are fond of. How long did it take?


As musicians ourselves, we find a lot of enjoyment in creating mixes just for fun in our spare time. A typical mix takes anywhere from 2 - 4 hours. Starting with choosing the song list to making the beats blend perfectly, and ending with a masterpiece.


What advice would you give a customer looking to hire a provider in your area of work?


Make sure they understand who you are and your expectations. The more you tell us up front the more we are able to handle it all when it's time to celebrate - letting you catch a break and enjoy!
